Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2024

Thank you for choosing NNSCalc. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our calculator application.

Information We Collect

NNSCalc is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect only the minimum amount of information necessary to provide our services:

  • Application Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app.
  • Calculation History: Your calculation history is stored locally on your device only. This information is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Memory Values: Any values stored in memory slots are kept locally on your device only.
  • Settings: Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To improve the user experience
  • To diagnose issues and fix bugs
  • To develop new features

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. We may share anonymous usage statistics with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our service, conducting our business, or serving our users, so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Storage and Security

Your calculation history and memory values are stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your calculation history at any time through the app's interface
  • Clear memory values at any time
  • Uninstall the application, which will remove all locally stored data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
